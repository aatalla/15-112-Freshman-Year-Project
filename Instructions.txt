Instructions:

*    Each player receives 13 cards to start with.

*    Each player can bid higher if he/she feel that they can collect a higher
     number of tricks with the set of cards they have (least: 7 tricks,
     highest: 13 tricks). The highest bidder gets to choose the trump suit.

*    The players opposite to each other form a team.

*    Each player plays a card on the table and the highest card or the
     highest trump suit (in case if it is played) collects the trick.

*    A player is only allowed to play the trump suit if he/she does not
     have the suit of the first card thrown in that turn.

*    You win the game by collecting the number of tricks bid first.
